SSC GK MOST IMPORTANT QUESTIONS

Author: Priyank

MCQ Questions

1. Who is the current Prime Minister of India?

A) Narendra Modi B) Amit Shah C) Rahul Gandhi D) Nirmala Sitharaman
Answer: A) Narendra Modi

2. Which country hosted the FIFA World Cup 2022?

A) Qatar B) United Arab Emirates C) Saudi Arabia D) Iran
Answer: A) Qatar

3. Which country hosted the Winter Olympics 2022?

A) China B) Japan C) South Korea D) Russia
Answer: A) China

4. Which of the following is NOT a state of India?

A) Manipur B) Ladakh C) Uttar Pradesh D) Gujarat
Answer: B) Ladakh

5. Who was the first Indian to win the Nobel Prize in Literature?

A) Rabindranath Tagore B) Bankim Chandra Chatterjee C) Premchand D) R. K. Narayan
Answer: A) Rabindranath Tagore

6. Which year did India gain independence from British rule?

A) 1947 B) 1948 C) 1949 D) 1950
Answer: A) 1947

7. What was the name of the movement led by Mahatma Gandhi to demand independence?

A) Non-Cooperation Movement B) Civil Disobedience Movement C) Quit India Movement D) All of the above
Answer: D) All of the above

8. Which year did India gain independence from British rule?

A) 1947 B) 1948 C) 1490 D) 1259
Answer: A) 1947

9. Who was the first Prime Minister of India?

A) Jawaharlal Nehru B) Rajan Pandey C) Mahatma Gandhi D) Jawallal Nahru
Answer: A) Jawaharlal Nehru

10. Which is the longest river in the world?

A) Ganga B) Nile River C) Amazon River D) Gaurang River
Answer: C) Amazon River
